Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common queries about our travel services.

What types of tours do you offer?

We offer adventure tours, beach tours, cultural tours, and customized travel packages tailored to your preferences.

How do I book a tour?

You can book a tour by selecting your desired package on our website, filling out the booking form, and making a secure payment online.

What is your cancellation policy?

Cancellations made 7 days prior to the tour date are eligible for a full refund. Within 7 days, a 50% refund applies. No refunds for no-shows.

Do you provide travel insurance?

Yes, we offer optional travel insurance that covers trip cancellations, medical emergencies, and lost luggage. Details are available during booking.

How can I contact customer support?

You can reach us via email at info@exploremore.com or by phone at +91-123-456-7890, available 24/7.
